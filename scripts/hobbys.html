Hobbys

One of the main things I love about living in The Netherlands is the freedom of cycling. I recently completed my longest cycle which was from Amsterdam to Rotterdam and am preparing to do a cycling tour from Groningen to Maastricht.

Architecture

While cycling, I get a prime view of all the architecture Europe has to offer. My favorite style of architecture being Gothic. A recent trip to Edinbrugh really blew my mind with their city design and detail put into their monument, buildings, and castles.
