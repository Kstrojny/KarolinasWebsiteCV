Motivation

You may be thinking to yourself, "Why would someone with minor coding experience want to turn their career path a full 180?" It was during the pandemic that started to get the ball rolling.

It began as a curiosity to invent a prompting device for my car which introduced me to the world of coding with Arduino and using robotics. I found great joy and accomplishment in my C++ projects and with no main focus in mind, I moved onto other areas like you can see here with HTML and CSS and making my way towards Javascript. Coding is something that I have been enjoying as a hobby and want to focus on that hobby to make it into my career.

There aren't many people out there enjoying what they do for work and my time in hospitality has come. I only need to enter through the door of IT whether that be data engineering, testing, or back end development. I've researched and heard of many success stories from people at Calco and they praise the opportunities they were given by being offered the option to complete the traineeship.

You would be adding someone to the team who has a background in communicating with teams of people and always problem solving. Someone who is familiar with managing large amounts of data and multitasking on multiple platforms. I know that my enthusiasm and goals are in line with what Calco wants and hope we can work together to accomplish our goals.

I thank you for taking your time exploring my little website and look forward to your response.
